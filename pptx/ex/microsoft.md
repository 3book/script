## Microsoft at a Glance

Microsoft Corporation is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Redmond, Washington. Founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975, Microsoft's mission is to **empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more**.

### Core Business

- Cloud Computing & AI Platform (Microsoft Azure)
- Productivity & Business Applications (Microsoft 365, Dynamics 365)
- Personal Computing (Windows, Surface, Xbox)
- Enterprise Software & Services
- Search & Advertising (Bing, LinkedIn)

### Key Metrics

- Revenue: $211+ billion (FY2024)
- Employees: 228,000+ worldwide
- Operations in 190+ countries
- Azure: #2 cloud provider globally
- GitHub: 100M+ developers

## Cloud & AI Leadership

![AI](./assets/tech-office.jpg){size:600*400,}

### Microsoft Azure

Azure is a comprehensive cloud computing platform offering 200+ products and cloud services. It enables organizations to build, deploy, and manage applications through Microsoft's global network of data centers.

### AI & Copilot

- **Copilot** — AI assistant integrated across Microsoft 365, Windows, Edge, and GitHub
- **Azure AI** — Enterprise AI platform with GPT-4, computer vision, and machine learning
- **Responsible AI** — Industry-leading framework for ethical AI development

## Product Ecosystem

### Windows & Devices

- Windows 11 powers 1.4+ billion devices worldwide
- Surface line of premium laptops, tablets, and accessories
- Mixed reality with HoloLens and Mesh platform

### Productivity Platform

- Microsoft 365 serves 400+ million paid seats
- Teams: 320+ million monthly active users
- SharePoint, OneDrive, Exchange form enterprise backbone

### Developer Tools

- Visual Studio and VS Code: #1 developer tools
- GitHub Copilot: AI pair programmer
- .NET and Azure DevOps for modern development

## Strategic Priorities

- **AI Transformation** — Embedding AI across every product line
- **Cloud First** — Azure as the engine of growth
- **Security** — $20B+ investment in cybersecurity over 5 years
- **Sustainability** — Carbon negative by 2030, water positive, zero waste
- **Gaming** — Xbox + Game Pass + Activision Blizzard ecosystem

## Corporate Responsibility

Microsoft has committed to becoming carbon negative by 2030 and removing all historical carbon emissions by 2050. The company's **AI for Good** initiative applies AI to solve global challenges in health, sustainability, accessibility, and humanitarian action.

The company also runs one of the world's largest philanthropy programs, with over $3.8 billion in technology donations to nonprofits annually through the Microsoft Philanthropies program.

## Summary

Microsoft continues to lead the technology industry through its three-pillar strategy: **Cloud + AI + Security**. With deep enterprise relationships, a vast developer ecosystem, and aggressive AI integration across its product portfolio, Microsoft is well-positioned for sustained growth in the coming decade.
